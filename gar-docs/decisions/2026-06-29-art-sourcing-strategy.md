# Art strategy: source creature models vs. custom model each one
_Resolved: prior to 2026-06-29 (exact date not recorded; logged retroactively)_

## Question
For a solo developer, custom-modeling dozens of brainrot creatures would be a major time
bottleneck. How should creature art be sourced for the rot system?

## Decision
Source ~50 brainrot creature models from the Roblox Creator Store instead of custom modeling.

## Why
This eliminated what would have been the single biggest content-production bottleneck for a solo
dev building a creature-collector game. It was a meaningful unblocking discovery mid-project.
Custom 3D modeling effort (Karson's time in Blender) is now reserved specifically for the
hangglider, which has no off-the-shelf equivalent and is core to the game's unique identity.

## Affects
[[rot-system]] (content supply), overall MVP timeline (removed a major risk)
