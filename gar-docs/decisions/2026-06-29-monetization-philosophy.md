# Monetization philosophy: acceleration vs. gatekeeping
_Resolved: prior to 2026-06-29 (exact date not recorded; logged retroactively)_

## Question
Should paid features in GAR gatekeep progression (pay to unlock), or only accelerate progress
free players can already achieve?

## Decision
Acceleration, not gatekeeping. Free players can fully progress through every system given enough
time; paid options only speed things up.

## Why
Preserves long-term player trust and retention, which matters more for a solo-dev game relying on
organic growth and word-of-mouth than short-term revenue maximization from a pay-to-win model.
Identified concrete levers consistent with this philosophy: crate cooldown reduction, crate
timers, and cosmetic skins (monetizing expression rather than power).
